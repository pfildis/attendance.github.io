What we do

Welcome to our revolutionary attendance management project that leverages the power of AI and face recognition techniques to streamline the process of taking attendance in the classroom. With our innovative system, bid farewell to traditional methods and embrace a modern, efficient, and accurate solution. At the heart of our project lies the implementation of a Siamese Network, specifically utilizing the SENet50 architecture. This advanced neural network model with weights that specifically trained for face recognition tasks, allowing us to achieve remarkable accuracy in identifying individuals.

To begin, we start with the premise that each student has only one photograph available, obtained from our institutional BİLSİS system. Leveraging this unique aspect, we leverage the Siamese Network to create embedded layers for each person's photograph, enabling us to establish distinct facial signatures.

Next, we employ the powerful YOLOV8 model as our face detector. This state-of-the-art algorithm excels at detecting and localizing faces within class photographs, providing us with the crucial initial step in our attendance-taking process. By extracting these detected faces, we gain a focused and refined dataset to work with. The real magic happens when we combine the embedded layers generated by the Siamese Network with the extracted faces from the YOLOV8 model. By comparing the embedded layers of individuals in our database with the embedded layers of the detected faces using the cosine distance method, we can confidently determine the identity of each individual in the class photographs. These techniques and algorithms ensures a robust and accurate attendance-taking system.
